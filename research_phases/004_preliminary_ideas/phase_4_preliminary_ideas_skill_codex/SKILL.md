# Phase 4 Preliminary Ideas

Read `../README.md`. Start from a Phase 2 gap and Phase 3 research question. Generate candidate mechanisms, not code or final proposals.

## Workflow

1. Write `problem-mechanism-map.md`: explain causes, assumptions, failure modes, and intervention points behind the question.
2. Explore at least four lenses when relevant: method transfer, method combination, mechanism redesign, problem reformulation, data/benchmark reframing, or theory-driven alternatives.
3. Create 5-10 distinct idea cards. Each card must state the target mechanism, why it could address the gap, assumptions, expected evidence, known risk, closest-work uncertainty, and one falsifying observation.
4. Screen cards for causal fit, internal consistency, distinctness, feasibility boundary, and likely prior-art overlap. Label novelty as `unsearched`, `partial`, or `searched`; never call it proven.
5. Produce `idea-portfolio.md`, `idea-comparison.csv`, and `phase4-handoff.md`. Keep several viable alternatives; do not score a final winner.

## Boundaries

Do not write an algorithm, training recipe, experiment plan, pilot result, or paper contribution claim. Treat any external search as evidence gathering and record sources. Hand Phase 5 only idea cards, uncertainties, and the mechanism map.
